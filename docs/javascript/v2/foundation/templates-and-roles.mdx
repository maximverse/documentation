## Introduction

If you wish to accept payments directly from your dashboard, without any code, it is possible as well!

Click on the `Paper` icon on the sidebar.

![Architecture](/docs/v2/paymentlinks1.png)

Click on the `Create payment link` button and choose your desired options. Done!
![Architecture](/docs/v2/paymentlinks2.png)
